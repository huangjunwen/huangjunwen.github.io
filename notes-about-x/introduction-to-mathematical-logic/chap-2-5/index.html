2.5 Additional Metatheorems and Derived Rules

一些额外的能让证明更加顺畅的派生规则1

2.5.1 Particularization Rule A4

如果 \(t\) 能自由替换 \(B(x)\) 中的 \(x\)，则 \((\forall x)B(x) \vdash B(t)\)， 特别地，\((\forall x)B(x) \vdash B(x)\)

这个规则可以直接从公理 A4 获得

2.5.2 Existential Rule E4

如果 \(t\) 能自由替换 \(B(x, t)\) 中的 \(x\)，令 \(B(t, t)\) 为 \(B(x, t)\) 中所有 \(x\) 的自由所在替换为 \(t\) 所得 （\(B(x, t)\) 中有可能有或没有 \(t\) 的存在），则有 \(B(t, t) \vdash (\exists x)B(x, t)\)， 特别地 \(B(t) \vdash (\exists x)B(x)\)，以及 \(B(x) \vdash (\exists x)B(x)\)

  1. \(\vdash (\forall x)\neg B(x, t) \Rightarrow \neg B(t, t)\) 由于 \(t\) 可以自由替换 \(B(x, t)\) 中的的 \(x\)，故 A4
  2. \(\vdash ((\forall x)\neg B(x, t) \Rightarrow \neg B(t, t)) \Rightarrow (\neg \neg B(t, t) \Rightarrow \neg (\forall x)\neg B(x, t))\) Lemma 1.11#e
  3. \(\neg \neg B(t, t) \Rightarrow \neg (\forall x)\neg B(x, t)\) MP 上述两公式
  4. \(B(t, t)\) Hyp
  5. \(B(t, t) \Rightarrow \neg \neg B(t, t)\) Lemma 1.11#b
  6. \(\neg \neg B(t, t)\) MP 上述两公式
  7. \(\neg (\forall x)\neg B(x, t)\) MP 3 和 6

以下这些派生规则非常有用

注：我将 negation 和 conditional 的放到前面来，因为在这个体系里，其他联结词是由它俩定义的

Conjunction elimination

\(B \wedge C \vdash B\)

  1. \(\neg B \Rightarrow (B \Rightarrow \neg C)\) Lemma 1.11#c
  2. \((\neg B \Rightarrow (B \Rightarrow \neg C)) \Rightarrow (\neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C) \Rightarrow \neg \neg B)\) Lemma 1.11#e
  3. \(\neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C) \Rightarrow \neg \neg B\) MP 上述两公式
  4. \(\neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C)\) Hyp
  5. \(B\) MP 上述两公式然后 Negation elimination

\(B \wedge C \vdash C\)

  1. \(\neg C \Rightarrow (B \Rightarrow \neg C)\) A1
  2. \((\neg C \Rightarrow (B \Rightarrow \neg C)) \Rightarrow (\neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C) \Rightarrow \neg \neg C)\) Lemma 1.11#e
  3. \(\neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C) \Rightarrow \neg \neg C\) MP 上述两公式
  4. \(\neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C)\) Hyp
  5. \(C\) MP 上述两公式然后 Negation elimination

\(\neg (B \wedge C) \vdash \neg B \vee \neg C\)

先证明 \(\neg (B \wedge C), \neg \neg B \vdash \neg C\)

  1. \(\neg \neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C)\) Hyp
  2. \(B \Rightarrow \neg C\) Negation elimination
  3. \(B\) Hyp 和 Negation elimination
  4. \(\neg C\) MP 上述两公式
  5. \(\neg (B \wedge C) \vdash \neg \neg B \Rightarrow \neg C\) 演绎定理，即 \(\neg B \vee \neg C\)

Conjunction introduction

\(B, C \vdash B \wedge C\)

  1. \(B \Rightarrow (\neg \neg C \Rightarrow \neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C))\) Lemma 1.11#f
  2. \(B\) Hyp
  3. \(\neg \neg C \Rightarrow \neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C)\) MP 上述两公式
  4. \(C\) Hyp
  5. \(\neg \neg C\) Negation introduction
  6. \(\neg (B \Rightarrow \neg C)\) MP 3 和 5

Conjunction commutative（额外）

\(B \wedge C \vdash C \wedge B\)

  1. \(B \wedge C\) Hyp
  2. \(B\) Conjunction elimination 1
  3. \(C\) Conjunction elimination 1
  4. \(C \wedge B\) Conjunction introduction 3 和 2

Disjunction elimination

\(B \vee C, \neg C \vdash B\)

  3. \(\neg C \Rightarrow \neg \neg B\) MP 上述两公式
  4. \(\neg C\) Hyp
  5. \(B\) MP 上述两公式然后 Negation elimination

  3. \(\neg C\) Conditional elimination 1

Disjunction introduction

\(B \vdash B \vee C\)

  1. \(B\) Hyp
  2. \(\neg \neg B\) Negation introduction
  3. \(\neg \neg B \Rightarrow (\neg B \Rightarrow C)\) Lemma 1.11#c

\(C \vdash B \vee C\)

  2. \(C \Rightarrow (\neg B \Rightarrow C)\) A1
  3. \(\neg B \Rightarrow C\) MP 上述两公式

Disjunction commutative（额外）

\(B \vee C \vdash C \vee B\)

  1. \(\neg B \Rightarrow C\) Hyp
  2. \((\neg B \Rightarrow C) \Rightarrow (\neg C \Rightarrow \neg \neg B)\) Lemma 1.11#e
  3. \(\neg C \Rightarrow \neg \neg B\) MP 上述两公式
  4. \(\neg C \Rightarrow B\) （因为 \(\neg C \Rightarrow \neg \neg B, \neg C \vdash B\)）

Biconditional elimination

\(B \Leftrightarrow C, B \vdash C\)

  1. \((B \Rightarrow C) \wedge (C \Rightarrow B)\) Hyp
  2. \(B \Rightarrow C\) Conjunction elimination 1
  3. \(B\) Hyp
  4. \(C\) MP 上述两公式

\(B \Leftrightarrow C, \neg B \vdash \neg C\)

  1. \((B \Rightarrow C) \wedge (C \Rightarrow B)\) Hyp
  2. \(C \Rightarrow B\) Conjunction elimination 1
  3. \(\neg B\) Hyp
  4. \(\neg C\) Conditional elimination 2 3

\(B \Leftrightarrow C, C \vdash B\)

  1. \((B \Rightarrow C) \wedge (C \Rightarrow B)\) Hyp
  2. \(C \Rightarrow B\) Conjunction elimination 1
  3. \(C\) Hyp
  4. \(B\) MP 上述两公式

\(B \Leftrightarrow C, \neg C \vdash \neg B\)

  1. \((B \Rightarrow C) \wedge (C \Rightarrow B)\) Hyp
  2. \(B \Rightarrow C\) Conjunction elimination 1
  3. \(\neg C\) Hyp
  4. \(\neg B\) Conditional elimination 2 3

\(B \Leftrightarrow C \vdash B \Rightarrow C\)

  1. \((B \Rightarrow C) \wedge (C \Rightarrow B)\) Hyp
  2. \(B \Rightarrow C\) Conjunction elimination 1

\(B \Leftrightarrow C \vdash C \Rightarrow B\)

  1. \((B \Rightarrow C) \wedge (C \Rightarrow B)\) Hyp
  2. \(C \Rightarrow B\) Conjunction elimination 1

Biconditional introduction

\(B \Rightarrow C, C \Rightarrow B \vdash B \Leftrightarrow C\)

用 Conjunction introduction 即可

Biconditional negation

\(B \Leftrightarrow C \vdash \neg B \Leftrightarrow \neg C\)

  1. \((B \Rightarrow C) \wedge (C \Rightarrow B)\) Hyp
  2. \(B \Rightarrow C\) Conjunction elimination 1
  3. \(\neg C \Rightarrow \neg B\) Conditional contrapositive 2
  4. \(C \Rightarrow B\) Conjunction elimination 1
  5. \(\neg B \Rightarrow \neg C\) Conditional contrapositive 4
  6. \(\neg B \Leftrightarrow \neg C\) Biconditional introduction 3 5

\(\neg B \Leftrightarrow \neg C \vdash B \Leftrightarrow C\)

证明类似上面

Proof by contradiction

如果证明 \(\Gamma, \neg B \vdash C \wedge \neg C\) 没有 Gen 用到 \(B\) 的自由变量，则 \(\Gamma \vdash B\)

  1. \(\neg C\) Conjunction elimination
  2. \(C\) Conjunction elimination
  3. \(\Gamma \vdash \neg B \Rightarrow \neg C\) Corollary 2.6 应用到 1
  4. \(\Gamma \vdash \neg B \Rightarrow C\) Corollary 2.6 应用到 2
  5. \(\vdash (\neg B \Rightarrow \neg C) \Rightarrow ((\neg B \Rightarrow C) \Rightarrow B)\) A3
  6. \(\Gamma \vdash B\) MP 两次 （3, 4, 5）

2.31 证明以下

  • a. \(\vdash (\forall x)(\forall y)A_1^2(x, y) \Rightarrow (\forall x)A_1^2(x, x)\)

    1. \((\forall x)(\forall y)A_1^2(x, y)\) Hyp
    2. \((\forall y)A_1^2(x, y)\) Rule A4
    3. \(A_1^2(x, x)\) Rule A4
    4. \((\forall x)A_1^2(x, x)\) Gen

    以上证明了 \((\forall x)(\forall y)A_1^2(x, y) \vdash (\forall x)A_1^2(x, x)\) 且前提是封闭公式故可用 DT 证得

  • b. \(\vdash (\forall x)B \vee (\forall x)C \Rightarrow (\forall x)(B \vee C)\)

    1. \(B \Rightarrow (\neg B \Rightarrow C)\) Lemma 1.11#c
    2. \((\forall x)(B \Rightarrow (\neg B \Rightarrow C))\) Gen 1
    3. \((\forall x)B \Rightarrow (\forall x)(\neg B \Rightarrow C)\) Ex 2.27#a 然后 MP 2
    4. \(C \Rightarrow (\neg B \Rightarrow C)\) A1
    5. \((\forall x)(C \Rightarrow (\neg B \Rightarrow C))\) Gen 4
    6. \((\forall x)C \Rightarrow (\forall x)(\neg B \Rightarrow C)\) Ex 2.27#a 然后 MP 5
    7. \((\forall x)B \vee (\forall x)C\) Hyp
    8. \((\forall x)(B \vee C)\) Rule Disjunction elimination 7/3/6
    9. \((\forall x)B \vee (\forall x)C \vdash (\forall x)(B \vee C)\) 1 ~ 8 所证明的
    10. Corollary 2.6

  • c. \(\vdash \neg (\exists x)B \Rightarrow (\forall x)\neg B\)

    1. \(\neg (\neg (\forall x)(\neg B))\) Hyp
    2. \((\forall x)(\neg B)\) Rule Negation elimination
    3. DT

  • d. \(\vdash (\forall x)B \Rightarrow (\forall x)(B \vee C)\)

    1. \((\forall x)B\) Hyp
    2. \(B\) Rule A4
    3. \(B \vee C\) Rule Disjunction introduction
    4. \((\forall x)(B \vee C)\) Gen
    5. 上一步 Gen 添加的量词不是 Hyp 中的自由变量，故可用 DT

  • e. \(\vdash (\forall x)(\forall y)(A_1^2(x, y) \Rightarrow \neg A_1^2(y, x)) \Rightarrow (\forall x)\neg A_1^2(x, x)\)

    1. \((\forall x)(\forall y)(A_1^2(x, y) \Rightarrow \neg A_1^2(y, x))\) Hyp
    2. \((\forall y)(A_1^2(x, y) \Rightarrow \neg A_1^2(y, x))\) Rule A4
    3. \(A_1^2(x, x) \Rightarrow \neg A_1^2(x, x)\) Rule A4
    4. \(\neg \neg A_1^2(x, x) \Rightarrow \neg A_1^2(x, x)\) Conditional contrapositive
    5. \(\neg \neg A_1^2(x, x) \Rightarrow \neg \neg A_1^2(x, x)\) Lemma 1.8
    6. \((\neg \neg A_1^2(x, x) \Rightarrow \neg \neg A_1^2(x, x)) \Rightarrow ((\neg \neg A_1^2(x, x) \Rightarrow \neg A_1^2(x, x)) \Rightarrow \neg A_1^2(x, x))\) A3
    7. \(\neg A_1^2(x, x)\) 对 6 连续 MP 5 和 4 即可
    8. \((\forall x)(\neg A_1^2(x, x))\) Gen
    9. DT

  • f. \(\vdash ((\exists x)B \Rightarrow (\forall x)C) \Rightarrow (\forall x)(B \Rightarrow C)\)

    1. \(\neg B \Rightarrow (B \Rightarrow C)\) Lemma 1.11#c
    2. \((\forall x)(\neg B \Rightarrow (B \Rightarrow C))\) Gen 1
    3. \((\forall x)(\neg B) \Rightarrow (\forall x)(B \Rightarrow C)\) Ex 2.27#a 然后 MP 2
    4. \(C \Rightarrow (B \Rightarrow C)\) A1
    5. \((\forall x)(C \Rightarrow (B \Rightarrow C))\) Gen 4
    6. \((\forall x)C \Rightarrow (\forall x)(B \Rightarrow C)\) Ex 2.27#a 然后 MP 5
    7. \((\forall x)(\neg B) \vee (\forall x)C\) Hyp
    8. \((\forall x)(B \Rightarrow C)\) Rule Disjunction elimination 7/3/6
    9. \((\exists x)B \Rightarrow (\forall x)C \vdash (\forall x)(B \Rightarrow C)\) 1 ~ 8 所证明的
    10. Corollary 2.6

  • g. \(\vdash (\forall x)(B \vee C) \Rightarrow (\forall x)B \vee (\exists x)C\)

    1. \((\forall x)(\neg B \Rightarrow C)\) Hyp
    2. \((\forall x)(\neg B \Rightarrow C) \Rightarrow ((\exists x)(\neg B) \Rightarrow (\exists x)C)\) Ex 2.27#b
    3. \((\exists x)(\neg B) \Rightarrow (\exists x)C\) MP 上述两公式
    4. \(\neg (\forall x)B\) Hyp
    5. \(\neg (\forall x)B \Rightarrow (\exists x)(\neg B)\) Ex 2.27#e
    6. \((\exists x)(\neg B)\) MP 上述两公式
    7. \((\exists x)C\) MP 3 和 6
    8. \((\forall x)(B \vee C), \neg (\forall x)B \vdash (\exists x)C\) 1 ~ 7 所证明的
    9. DT 两次

  • h. \(\vdash (\forall x)(A_1^2(x, x) \Rightarrow (\exists y)A_1^2(x, y))\)

    2. \((\exists y)A_1^2(x, y)\) Rule E4

  • k. \(\vdash ((\forall x)(\forall y)(B(x, y) \Rightarrow B(y, x)) \wedge (\forall x)(\forall y)(\forall z)(B(x, y) \wedge B(y, z) \Rightarrow B(x, z))) \Rightarrow (\forall x)(\forall y)(B(x, y) \Rightarrow B(x, x))\)

    3. \((\forall x)(\forall y)(\forall z)(B(x, y) \wedge B(y, z) \Rightarrow B(x, z))\) conjunction elimination 1
    4. \(B(x, y)\) Hyp
    5. \(B(x, y) \Rightarrow B(y, x)\) Rule A4 2
    6. \(B(y, x)\) MP 上述两公式
    7. \(B(x, y) \wedge B(y, x) \Rightarrow B(x, x)\) Rule A4 3

    12. \(\vdash (\forall x)(\forall y)(B(x, y) \Rightarrow B(y, x)) \wedge (\forall x)(\forall y)(\forall z)(B(x, y) \wedge B(y, z) \Rightarrow B(x, z)) \Rightarrow (\forall x)(\forall y)(B(x, y) \Rightarrow B(x, x))\) DT（Gen 的量词不是 Hyp 的自由变量）

    2. \((\exists y)A_1^2(x, y)\) Rule E4 1
